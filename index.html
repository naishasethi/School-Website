campus life

Science Park

Innovative teaching tool to inculcate scientific temper. Installed with colourful interactive scientific gadgets. Cluster of open air science gadgets covering Biology, Chemistry, Sound light & Mechanics

Libary (Senior and Junior)

Senior Libary: Well stocked and updated library. Latest software to facilitate searching books. Reference work with Internet facilities. Books, magazines and journals on a wide range of subjects. Educational CDs. Enlightening sessions by eminent resource persons. An array of activities like Quiz, Book discussion and Book Reading to name a few.
Junior Libary:Well stocked and updated library. Latest software to facilitate finding books.

Sport Facilities

Sprawling playground. Special areas earmarked for different games. Professional coaches. Facilities for various sports – Cricket, Basketball, Football, Skating, Hockey, Karate, Table Tennis, Swimming. Students participate in national and international coaches. Expert guidance provided by professional coaches. Junior & Senior Sports Day celebrated on an annual basis.

whats new

  • Founder's Day

    Date: November 19, 2019 Birthday celebrations of visionary entrepreneur Dr. Ashok K. Chauhan, Founder President, Amity Universe commenced with lamp lighting by Dr. (Mrs) Amita Chauhan, Chairperson, Amity Group of Schools and RBEF, amidst the chanting of shlokas. A short movie based on life and times of the Founder President highlighting his vision, challenges and achievements were showcased. Students presented an enthralling dance. Prizes were given to the winners of ‘Creative Edge,’ an event comprising host of activities. Meritorious students and achievers in academic and non-academic activities were felicitated with badges, scholarships and trophies.


  • Prerna Diwas Celebrations

    April 22-27, 2019 Students and staff of the school celebrated birthday of their beloved Chairperson as ‘Prerna Diwas’ which also marked the culmination of a weeklong ‘Earth day’ celebration. A havan was organised on the occasion to pray for well being and happiness. During entire week children participated in different activities focusing on saving earth. These included posters making, bookmark making, limerick composition, JAM sessions, essay writing, power point presentation, creating vertical gardens, movie screening sessions, making bird house and bird feeder, group discussions, etc.
